Shame

Description

Shame is a bad feel after doing a bad action.

Would you like to cook a Shame for your own consumption? Limited information is available about this topic, but we've assembled what is known.

Ingredients

  • 1 ham
  • 1 uppercase letter S
  • 1 lowercase letter e

Steps

  1. Hot glue your uppercase S to the left side of your ham.
  2. Hot glue your lowercase e to the right side of your ham.
  3. Show another person in your home what you have done.
